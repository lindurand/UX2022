Fixed Assets Online

I was the lead UX designer and the information architect for this online CPA application. This product aims to provides solutions for business owners to track the varied lifecycles of the assets and get the tax refund. No IT infrastructure needed other than a computer connected to the Internet. Anytime, anywhere access to the data. And no need to worry about upgrades and backups.

The Design Process

Drawing the concept of the product. Figure out who is the user and how the product works.

Create customer journey mapping and user personas.

Base on the comp, put all the pages together as a sitemap.

Create flows for all the interaction.

Put together detailed wireframe with annotations.
