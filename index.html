BaseSpace™ Sequence Hub

Simplify your bioinformatics with an economical and powerful computing environment to manage, analyze, and share data. BaseSpace Sequence Hub is a direct extension of your Illumina instruments. Encrypted data flow from the instrument into BaseSpace Sequence Hub, enabling you to manage and analyze your data easily with a curated set of analysis apps. BaseSpace Sequence Hub, powered by Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Global Access and Data Sharing

Challenge:

For creating a platform to handle multiple subscriptions and billings for enterprise and professional users from US, EU and China, The system needed to be flexible enough to handle varying pricing logics in different regions. Monitization for all types of accounts

TruSight™ Software Suite

A cloud-based software solution that can manage, analyze and interpret data automatically. From laboratory information management and data storage, to analysis and interpretation, this new, innovative solution can improve clinic’s reporting workflows for researching inherited disease and automate genomic interpretation.


From concept to release, I designed the TruSight Software Suite, an unique end-to-end clinical lab application. It was released and announced in JP Morgan Healthcare Conference and got great reviews from Beta customers. I got nominated for Illumina Innovation Award. With great features, TruSight Software Suite helped beta customers solved many unsolved cases.


JPM Presentation

Overview of Concept

Next-generation sequencing (NGS) is becoming commonplace when seeking a diagnosis for rare genetic disease. This can create a bottleneck when analyzing and interpreting the billions of base pairs and millions of variants in the genome. Introducing a new suite of software tools to streamline this important analysis. Designed to provide comprehensive information seamlessly at your fingertips—the TruSight™ Software Suite enables informed analysis and empowered interpretation.

User Research

Challenge:

No Competitors.

It was positioned to be an end-to-end clinical solution product, which is very unique solution in the market. Since no one created it before and the data is very complcated. We did a lot of user research to refine the scope, personas and process flows.

Personas

User researcher and I performed research on the clinical lab working process with internal and external customers. Based on the research data, I created the journey map, process flows, list of user needs, main personas, matrix of terms, list of important features, and messages system.

Customer journey map

Product manager and I identified the major selling points based on the pain-points and required features. We convinced the stakeholders to adjust the original scope to meet users’ needs.
Within 7 weeks, based on existing customer research, I proposed a clickable prototype, planned and conducted 6 customer interviews with external lab leaders regarding the proposed flows, refined designs based on customer research and delivered screen designs for product's first release in mid-May.

Chart for Data Flows, Main Personas and IA

I analysed all the requested features, and found the connecting points which allow the case management portal to sync-up the information from all the user activities, enabling the lab to track project process and get notification about turnaround time.

Then I mapped out the eTRP portal’s client billing and sample collection process, building the connection between eTRP and the case management portal which simplifying the communication between the clinic and labs.

Wireframe and annotations

UI designer and I created over 70 pages of wireframes within one month and we collaborated with developers in 6 scrum teams to start development process. During this process, the app connected to more and more services. I created short-term and long-term Machine Learning strategy and implemented SpliceAI and PrimateAI to speed up the curation speed.

Prototypes

I used InVision to create clickable prototypes to test in front of users. This helped developers to understand user case scenarios and UI interaction.

Customer Feedbacks

Product received very positive feedbacks from the beta users and internal lab users.
I got nominated for Illumina Innovation Award.
